Balancing Wisdom: Unveiling My Diverse Horizons in Extra Curricular Activities.

I believe in embracing a well-rounded lifestyle. In addition to my love for coding and technology, I actively participate in various extracurricular activities that not only contribute to my personal growth but also allow me to give back to the community. One of my favorite pursuits is teaching tech to aspiring juniors, sharing my knowledge and experience to inspire the next generation of developers. Additionally, I engage with various tech-related organizations that provide a platform for networking, learning, and collaborating with like-minded individuals.

. Hosting Hands-On Practical Workshops.

During my junior year of study, I had the wonderful opportunity to organize and conduct several enriching workshops, one of which was a highly successful Web Development workshop tailored specifically for sophomores. Over four intensive days, I guided a batch of 100 students in creating a fully functional Banking web application. The workshop covered a comprehensive curriculum, starting from fundamentals and building knowledge step by step.

Throughout the workshop, my focus was on imparting essential skills, teaching participants how to code in HTML, CSS, and utilize Bootstrap for responsive web design . Additionally, I introduced them to the world of databases by teaching SQL from scratch, enabling seamless data storage and retrieval. To make their web applications dynamic and interactive, I shared my expertise in hosting web servers and creating robust classical backends using PHP.

The resounding success of this workshop led to its high demand. In my senior year, I conducted the Web Development workshop once again, continuing the tradition of empowering and nurturing the next generation of web developers.
